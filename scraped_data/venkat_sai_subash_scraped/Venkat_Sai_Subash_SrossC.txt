May 20,1992 

 First satellite successfully orbited by ASLV
Mission 	Experimental
Weight 	106.1 kg
Onboard power 	45 Watts
Communication 	S-band and VHF
Stabilization 	Spin stabilized with a Magnetic Torquer and Magnetic Bias Control
Payload 	Gamma Ray Burst (GRB) experiment & Retarding Potential Analyser (RPA) experiment
Launch date 	May 20,1992
Launch site 	SHAR Centre, Sriharikota, India
Launch vehicle 	Augmented Satellite Launch Vehicle (ASLV)
Orbit 	267 x 391 km
Mission life 	Two months (Re-entered on July15,1992)
